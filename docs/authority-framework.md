# Authority Framework

## Overview

Authority Nodes represent the highest trust tier within APP federation.

Authority status is earned through verifiable evidence and operational maturity.

---

# Purpose

Authority Nodes help establish trust across independent federation participants.

They may:

- Issue authority attestations
- Participate in federation governance
- Contribute trust signals
- Support ecosystem integrity

---

# Requirements

Potential requirements include:

- Verified domain ownership
- Active federation participation
- Cryptographic compliance
- Proven operational history
- Transparent governance

---

# Responsibilities

Authority Nodes should:

- Act transparently
- Publish federation information
- Maintain security standards
- Avoid abuse of authority

---

# Limitations

Authority status does not grant control over the protocol.

APP remains an open and federated system.

Authority influences trust but does not create centralization.

---

# Future Development

The Authority Framework will evolve as federation adoption increases.

Community participation is encouraged.
